About

I am a Cloud Platform Architect with over 17 years of experience designing and operating scalable cloud infrastructure and automation platforms. My work focuses on building reliable, secure, and highly automated systems that enable engineering teams to move faster while maintaining operational stability.

I specialize in Kubernetes platforms, infrastructure as code with Terraform, CI/CD systems, cloud architecture, and reliability engineering across AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. Throughout my career I have led initiatives that transformed complex manual operations into streamlined automated platforms, significantly improving deployment velocity and system reliability.
